Common Questions

Before you reach out,
here's what people ask.

How quickly can you start?

After our initial consultation and scoping, most projects can begin within 1–2 weeks. Urgent requirements like security incidents are handled immediately.

Do you work with small businesses?

Absolutely. We work with businesses of all sizes — from startups building their first website to established companies modernising their entire infrastructure.

What does the consultation involve?

A focused 30-minute call where we learn about your business, current setup, and goals. We'll give you honest advice and a clear picture of how we can help — no obligations.

Are your services available nationally?

Yes. We operate across South Africa and serve clients remotely where on-site presence isn't required. We also handle on-site for businesses in our operational regions.

What if I only need one service?

No problem at all. While we offer bundled solutions, we're happy to assist with a single service area. Many clients start with one and expand as they see results.

Do you offer ongoing contracts?

Yes. We offer monthly retainer packages for IT support, security monitoring, and website maintenance. These can be customised to fit your specific needs and budget.
